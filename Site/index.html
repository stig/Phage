Phage

Phage is a two-player abstract strategy game for Mac OS X. Pit your mind against the machine: can you beat it? The best way to find out is to download this Phage disk image and start playing!

Get in touch!

Mail me at stig@brautaset.org if you find a bug, have an idea for improvement, or simply love playing the game. See also my contact page for other ways to get in touch with me.
